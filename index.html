Queen Tribute Page

Bio

Queen is a British rock band formed in London in 1970. Members include Freddie Mercury (lead vocals, piano), Brian May (guitar, vocals), John Deacon (bass guitar), and Roger Taylor (drums, vocals). Some of their most popular hits include “We Will Rock You”, “Another One Bites the Dust”, and “Bohemian Rhapsody”. Despite Mercury’s tragic death in 1991, Queen remains popular to this day.

Having sold over 170 million records, Queen is highly regarded as one of the world’s best-selling music artists of all time. The band has received numerous awards, including the Grammy Lifetime achievement award in 2018.
